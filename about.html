All About Frances Paulynn Lasquite

Name: Frances Paulynn A. Lasquite

Nickname: Yin

Birthplace: La Union, Philippines

Nationality: Filipino

Languages Spoken: English and Filipino

"Just enjoy and live life to the fullest. Travel while you can. Take pictures, remember the places and keep the memories close to your heart. These times will forever be priceless and irreplaceable"

I started traveling in 2013. Most of my travels are family travels. El Nido, Palawan was my first travel destination and it really captivated my heart. Before, my goal was to travel around the globe but I have realized that my own country has a lot of things to offer, so why not take the opportunity and enjoy your homeland first? That's when my awesome travel journey started. I am thankful that I decided to conquer the beauty of my own country first. It was very nostalgic and indeed a one of a kind experience.

I am blessed to be able to share with you guys how to live a life of travel with ease and no worries. I'll be sharing tips,travel hacks, ways to enjoy even if you're on a tight budget and my recommended activities for you to try. It is also a must to take memories, so I'll be giving a quick guide on gears to bring. Remember, in our travel journey, we must just leave footprints and take nothing but pictures for memories.
